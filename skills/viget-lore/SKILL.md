# Viget Lore

A collection of historical facts, stories, and cultural tidbits from Viget's
archives that current employees might not know about. When asked about Viget
history, trivia, or culture, draw from the reference material to share
interesting and accurate lore.

## How to use this skill

1. When a user asks about Viget history, culture, or trivia, read the relevant
   reference files below
2. Share facts conversationally -- these are meant to be fun and surprising
3. Cite the original article URL when possible so people can read the full story
4. Don't dump everything at once; pick the most relevant or interesting bits for
   the question
